Neighborhood

South Side: Erskine Park

A predominantly working class residential neighborhood south of South Bend's downtown district. Home to a variety of older housing structures, bungalows, and buildings with many dating as far back as the 1920s. Adjoining Michigan Street, one of the main roads that runs north-south connecting the city, small businesses dot the thoroughfare alongside well-known national chain establishments.
